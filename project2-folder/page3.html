drown

how do you save someone
who wants to stay underwater?

someone who cares not for sinking to the bottom,
for holding their breath until their head nearly explodes,
someone who lets the water fill their lungs
and allows it to pull them down deeper into the sea.

throw them a life line all you want,
but try as you might they will not swim back up.

no matter how hard you wish,
you can’t force them to take another breath
if they decide that saline is better than oxygen.

should you keep your arm extended to pull them from the water,
they will take it and claw you down to its depths,
nails puncturing cold skin, salt filling fresh wounds,
as they also sink beneath the surface.

trying to save someone who chooses to stay underwater,
someone who does not care to take another breath of fresh air,
is as good as attempting to resuscitate a man
who has already had his lungs filled with water.

you cannot blame yourself for their decision,
can’t claim that your strength or speed failed you,
as it was their choice to refuse your outstretched arms.

the only choice you must make
is if you will drown with them
or let them learn to swim.
